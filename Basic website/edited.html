Reinefjorden

Reinefjorden is a fjord of the Vestfjord
in the municipality of Mosken in Nordland,
which extends almost
three kilometers in the eastern
direction from the inlet
between Hamnøya in the
northeast and Andøya,
north of Reine , in the southwest.

The Northern Lights

When the northern lights – Aurora Borealis
are strong and bright, get ready
for the show of your life
as the lights dance across the sky
from horizon to horizon in display
of nature that I can only describe as
truly breathtaking.

The Bunes Beach

Bunes beach is one of the most beautiful places on the islands.
It is inaccessible by car.
You will have to sail on the ferry and walk.
The trek is very easy.
What is there to see and specific points.
You will not leave the beach without
replenishing your collection with various pictures.
You can start shooting on the way to the beach.
Once in the pass, turn around and you will have
a view of the fjord and the village.
